Services

Comprehensive home inspection

We’ll conduct a thorough, non-invasive physical inspection designed to assist in evaluating the overall condition of the dwelling. The inspection will be based on observations of the visible and apparent condition of the structure and its components on the date of the inspection. The condition will be described in a comprehensive written report. The inspection may include activating the home’s heating and cooling equipment, operating plumbing fixtures, testing accessible electrical outlets and fixtures, and operating a representative sampling of doors and windows. We’ll also conduct a visual inspection of the roof, crawl spaces,walls and drainage adjacent to the home.

Radon inspection

We’ll test your home for levels of the radioactive gas radon and present recommendations for mitigating the radon levels if needed.

Mold inspection

We’ll inspect and assess your property for signs of a mold problem, which can be dangerous to the health of you and your loved ones. If mold is found, we have the training, equipment, and expertise to remediate your mold infestation.

Termite inspection

We’ll inspect your home or potential home and provide you with a report outlining any existing damage or infestations and areas that are liable to incur future infestations.

Sewer scope inspection

We’ll inspection your property’s sewer line to determine its condition and whether it’s functioning as designed.

Energy audit

We’ll conduct an audit to help you assess how much energy your home uses and evaluate what measures you can take to improve its efficiency.

Wind mitigation inspection

Wind mitigation is a strategy designed to limit the amount of wind damage that can be inflicted on a structure. We’ll help determine which improvements are necessary to keep your home safe from wind.

Roof certification

We’ll inspect the condition of your roof and complete the certification form as required by your insurer.

4-point inspection

We’ll conduct a 4-point inspection, which focuses on four main areas of interest in a home: HVAC (heating, ventilation and air Conditioning), electrical wiring and panels, plumbing connections and fixtures, and the roof.
